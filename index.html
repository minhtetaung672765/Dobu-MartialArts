Facilites For You

For you better experience fitness and martial arts within our area.


Matted martial arts area

Our large, matted martial arts area provides a safe and spacious environment for practicing various martial arts disciplines. The high-quality mats ensure safety during intense training sessions and competitions.

Fully-equipped gym

Our fully-equipped gym features state-of-the-art fitness equipment to support all your training needs. From free weights and resistance machines to cardio equipment, our gym caters to all fitness levels.

After a rigorous training session, relax and rejuvenate in our sauna. The sauna helps in muscle recovery, detoxification, and stress relief, providing a perfect end to your workout.
