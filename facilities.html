State-of-the-Art Facilities

At DoBu Martial Arts, we pride ourselves on offering top-notch facilities designed to enhance your training experience. Our large, matted martial arts area and the fully-equipped gym caters to all your fitness needs, while our sauna and steam room provide the perfect spaces for relaxation and recovery.

 Our facilities are designed not just to train you, but to transform you. Step in, and step up your game. 

- OUR FACILITIES -





Large, matted martial arts area

Our large, matted martial arts area provides a safe and spacious environment for practicing various martial arts disciplines. The high-quality mats ensure safety during intense training sessions and competitions. The area is designed to accommodate both individual and group training, allowing for versatile use by our members.

Fully-equipped gym

Our fully-equipped gym features state-of-the-art fitness equipment to support all your training needs. From free weights and resistance machines to cardio equipment, our gym caters to all fitness levels. Our dedicated staff is available to assist with personalized workout plans and ensure you get the most out of your training.

Sauna

After a rigorous training session, relax and rejuvenate in our sauna. The sauna helps in muscle recovery, detoxification, and stress relief, providing a perfect end to your workout. The calming environment of the sauna promotes overall wellness and enhances the recovery process, making it an integral part of our facility.

Steam Room

Experience the therapeutic benefits of our steam room, which is designed to aid in muscle relaxation and improve circulation. The steam room helps to open up airways, enhance skin health, and promote a sense of tranquility. It's an excellent way to unwind and soothe your body after an intense training session.

Changing and Shower Room

Our changing and shower facilities are clean, modern, and spacious, ensuring you have a comfortable experience before and after your workouts. Equipped with secure lockers, ample space, and high-quality showers, these facilities provide everything you need to freshen up and prepare for your day or evening activities.
